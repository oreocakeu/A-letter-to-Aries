II.

Even if I wasn’t the first person you liked, or the first one to ask you, I still believe I should be the only one you choose, with no other options. Like, pleaseeeeeeeeee, Okay?
"The most important things are the hardest to say, because words diminish them."
✦

III.

And no—I didn't love you at first because of your face, your clothes, your style, your mindset, or the way you interact. No. I’m just comfortable with you—so much so that I can’t even breathe normally without you. Well, yes, I already admired you for your crafts, but that isn't just it. I know those things wouldn't be enough to answer the question I asked myself a long time ago: "Why do I like you?" Maybe it's mainly because it makes me cry when you aren't here and you aren't with me. Can that be enough? I can’t function properly without you—not when you’re silent and grumpy, and not when we’re both being avoidant in our own ways and for our own reasons.

I am forever grateful for your growth and your braveness. You’ve come so far, and you deserve to walk much further, achieving more and more until you can finally rest without having to worry about anything. I hope that rest will be with me, right beside you. You’re still a baby, though, so continue enjoying life as it is. Even if I’m far away, I’m still with you, as always. I’d always choose to be with you—and I’d always choose you.

I love you, sweetheart.
